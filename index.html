Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and process.

What platform do you use for hosting my website? +

We leverage robust and modern platforms like Netlify to host client websites. This ensures excellent speed, security, and reliability with global content delivery. All hosting is fully managed by Johnson Website Solutions, so you don't have to worry about the technical details – we handle it all for you as part of your hosting plan.

Who owns my website and hosting account? +

You, the client, are the owner of your website content and domain name. For hosting, we typically set up and manage the account on platforms like Netlify to provide you with a seamless, stress-free experience. We ensure you have access to your site's resources and will always provide you with the necessary credentials for your Netlify account. Our goal is transparency and your complete control over your assets. Should you ever choose to move your site or manage hosting yourself, we facilitate a smooth handover. Please see our Service Terms for more details.

What if my website needs more resources than the standard hosting plan offers? +

Our standard $10/month hosting plan is designed to generously accommodate the needs of most small business websites, leveraging the free or low-cost tiers of platforms like Netlify. If your website's traffic or resource requirements (e.g., extensive use of serverless functions, very high bandwidth) grow to a point where they exceed these standard provisions and incur additional costs from the underlying platform, we will discuss this with you transparently. Any such additional costs would be passed on to you, but only after prior notification and agreement. Our aim is to keep your hosting affordable and predictable.

How does the "AI Website Design Consultant" work? +

Our AI Design Consultant tool uses Google's Gemini API. You provide details about your business, goals, and preferences through a simple form. The AI then analyzes this information to generate initial ideas for your website's structure, key homepage content, calls to action, and visual style. You can review and edit these suggestions before submitting them to us. It's a collaborative way to kickstart the design process and ensure your website vision is clear from the outset.

What does "all work done by me personally" mean for my project? +

It means that I, Randy Johnson, will be your direct point of contact and the primary developer working on your website. This ensures consistency, direct accountability, and a deep understanding of your project from start to finish. It allows me to combine my technical skills with my business background (BBA) to deliver a site that truly aligns with your goals. No outsourcing means you get a dedicated, personal service.

Hosting Policy & Service Terms Summary

Key information about our website development and hosting services. For full details, please refer to your individual service agreement.

Website Hosting

Johnson Website Solutions (JWS) provides fully managed web hosting for client websites. We leverage industry-leading, robust, and modern third-party platforms, such as Netlify, to ensure high performance, security (including SSL certificates), and reliability for your website. Our $10/month hosting fee covers the management of this service, standard resource usage, and performance monitoring. We handle the technical setup and ongoing maintenance so you can focus on your business.

Account Ownership & Access

While JWS typically sets up and manages the hosting account on platforms like Netlify for client convenience and efficient support, you (the client) retain ownership of your website content and domain name. We are committed to transparency and will provide you with the login credentials for your specific site's hosting account (e.g., your Netlify site instance). This ensures you have access and control. Should you decide to terminate our hosting services, we will facilitate a smooth handover of all necessary files and assist with migration where feasible (additional service fees may apply for complex migrations).

Billing for Hosting & Platform Costs

Our standard monthly hosting fee is designed to cover typical resource usage on platforms like Netlify, often within their free or low-cost tiers. If your website's traffic, bandwidth, or use of advanced platform features (e.g., extensive serverless functions, add-ons beyond standard provisions) consistently exceeds these typical allowances and incurs additional costs from the third-party hosting platform, JWS will:

  • Notify you in advance of any potential overages or required plan upgrades on the platform.
  • Discuss options and seek your approval before any additional charges are incurred.
  • Pass through these direct platform costs to you, in addition to your standard JWS hosting management fee.
Our goal is to provide predictable and affordable hosting, with clear communication regarding any necessary adjustments due to your site's growth.

Service Agreement

Upon engagement, you will receive a detailed service agreement outlining the full scope of work, deliverables, payment terms, timelines, policies regarding revisions and ongoing support, and specific terms related to hosting and third-party services. We encourage you to review this agreement carefully. This summary provides an overview, but the formal service agreement will govern our engagement.

Security & Maintenance

We take security seriously. Our hosting management includes implementing SSL certificates and monitoring performance. While we strive to maintain a secure environment using reputable platforms, clients are responsible for the security of their own content and login credentials. For client-managed Netlify accounts, ensuring strong, unique passwords is vital. JWS is not liable for security breaches resulting from compromised client credentials or vulnerabilities within client-managed content.
